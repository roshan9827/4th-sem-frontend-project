Patients' families in underdeveloped nations like Nepal bear the responsibility and worry of managing and transporting blood.

Why We Should
Donate Blood;

  • Saves Lives
  • Reduces Cardiovascular Risk
  • Balances Iron Levels
  • Stimulates Blood Cell Production
  • Enhances Immune System Health
  • Screening for Health Issues
  • Community Contribution
  • Emotional Satisfaction
  • Improved Cardiovascular Health
  • Quick Recovery
  • It takes 36 hours to naturally replace blood lost from the body

“Donate Blood Because You Never Know.How Helpful It Might Be To Someone.”

    Side effects of blood donation
  1. Bruising or Bleeding at the Needle Insertion Site
  2. Dizziness or Lightheadedness
  3. Nausea, Fatigue
  4. Vasovagal Reaction
  5. Arm Pain or Numbness
  6. Allergic Reactions
  7. Iron Deficiency
